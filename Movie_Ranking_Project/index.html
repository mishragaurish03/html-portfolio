The Best Movies of all Time for me

My Top 3 Movies

Gadar

This movie shows the power of love. This movie shows how a lover crosses all the boundaries of religion and countries to protect his wife.

Lagaan

This movie is a true inspiration for all of us. This movie shows how self determination helps us to achieve anything in our life. It also shows the power unity.

Anand

This movie is mine all time favourite. This movie shows how a man who is about to die lives his life without any fear of death.
